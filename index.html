Welcome to KOLEX WEBSITE

We are corporate technology trainers (aka “Advyzers”) with over two decades of experience teaching Microsoft 365 applications to tens of thousands of professionals nationwide in live, virtual or blended learning environments. We specialize in helping you to enhance and streamline time spent at the computer. For us, every training engagement is full of exciting possibilities, and we think you will get caught up in that enthusiasm, too! When you partner with Advyze, we will work with you to carefully define your training goals and craft a plan to achieve and exceed your objectives. After your training sessions are complete, we will provide ongoing support to help you to synthesize your newly learned skills, and every student is invited to receive our “Advyzer Tips of the Day”, which extend and deepen the classroom experience!
